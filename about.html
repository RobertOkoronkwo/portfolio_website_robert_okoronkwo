My Story

I started my career as a graphic designer, which allowed me to gain a great deal of experience giving value to clients building or rebranding their business. In regards to educational background, I have achieved a Masters in Business Analytics and Data Science, which has aided in my knowledge of data research, competitive analysis, system operations, and report metrics. Over the years, I realized my career passion aligned fully in UX/UI Design based on prior experience in visual design and analysis. Soon after receiving my Masters Degree (Dec 2020), I devoted the next 9-12 months in a UX Bootcamp. It took a considerable amount of time to complete, but my drive to learn and finish what I start pushed me through.

My attention to detail, meeting deadlines, and time management are my top quality traits. I like to work within inclusive teams to receive feedback from developers or stakeholders involved.
